Admissions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Children seeking admission to class LKG should have completed 3 years of age.
  • A corresponding age is fixed for the subsequent classes.
  • Pupils who join the school in standard 11 and above should submit a Transfer Certificate from the previous school.
  • If the school is outside the state T.S., the Transfer Certificate should be countersigned by the Education Officer of the respective state

Important Notes

  • The admission is strictly based on the merit obtained by the candidates in the entrance test conducted by the School.
  • The Principal is not bound to assign any reason for rejecting an application.

Required Documents

  • Original Birth certificate together with a true copy.
  • Transfer certificate from the school last attended.
  • Two copies of passport size photograph
  • Aadhar xerox copy

Fee Structure

  • Fees are payable for all 12 months.
  • Admission fee must be paid at the time of admission.
  • Fee once paid is non-refundable, even if the student does not attend school.
  • Detailed fee structure can be obtained from the school office.
