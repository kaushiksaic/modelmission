About MMHS

THE FEAR OF GOD IS THE BEGINNING OF KNOWLEDGE

The MMHS Legacy

“The fear of God is the beginning of knowledge.” Established in 1988, Model Mission High School is a Christian Minority co-educational institution managed by the Oxford Educational Society and recognized by the Government of Telangana. We follow the State Board syllabus and prepare students for the SSC Examination. At MMHS, we embrace every child’s unique potential. Our approach focuses on academic excellence, strong values, and holistic development through a wide range of enriching activities. We aim to prepare students for a dynamic, competitive world by fostering self-discipline, intellectual strength, and physical fitness.

⭐ OUR MISSION
Our mission is to provide every student with the tools, facilities, and opportunities needed for all-round development. We aim to mold confident, responsible individuals who shine in whichever path they choose — becoming tomorrow’s leaders, thinkers, and achievers.
